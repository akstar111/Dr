Dr. Sudharsan, MBBS, DNB in Internal Medicine, is a highly experienced and dedicated medical professional with over 30 years of expertise in the field of healthcare. Specializing in Internal Medicine and Geriatric Care, Dr. Sudharsan has been committed to providing comprehensive and compassionate care to patients of all ages, with a particular focus on the elderly.

With his extensive knowledge and skills, Dr. Sudharsan has become a trusted physician, known for his thorough diagnostic approach and personalized treatment plans. His experience in managing complex medical conditions, along with his empathetic approach, ensures that his patients receive the highest standard of care and support throughout their medical journey.

Dr. Sudharsan’s dedication to improving the quality of life for his patients, especially senior citizens, is evident in his continued efforts to stay at the forefront of advancements in geriatric care and internal medicine. Whether it's managing chronic conditions or providing preventive care, he strives to make a positive impact on every patient’s health and well-being.
