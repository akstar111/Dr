Dr. Sudharsan, MBBS, DNB in Internal Medicine, is a seasoned medical professional with 30 years of experience in the healthcare field. Specializing in Internal Medicine, Diabetology, and Geriatric Care, he has dedicated his career to providing exceptional, patient-centered care for individuals of all ages, with a particular focus on managing chronic conditions like diabetes and addressing the unique healthcare needs of the elderly.

As a certified diabetologist, Dr. Sudharsan has extensive expertise in diagnosing, managing, and treating diabetes, helping patients achieve better control over their condition and preventing complications. His deep knowledge of Internal Medicine allows him to handle a wide range of medical issues, ensuring comprehensive and effective treatment for his patients.

In addition, Dr. Sudharsan has a strong passion for Geriatric Care, offering specialized care to older adults and addressing the challenges they face as they age. His compassionate approach, combined with his vast experience, allows him to provide individualized care that enhances the quality of life for senior citizens.

With 30 years of experience, Dr. Sudharsan is recognized for his thorough diagnostic skills, commitment to continuous learning, and a patient-first approach. His expertise and dedication make him a trusted physician for those seeking high-quality care in Internal Medicine, Diabetology, and Geriatric Care.
